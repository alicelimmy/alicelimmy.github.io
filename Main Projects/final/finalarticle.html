    • HOW TO DO BRUNCH RIGHT IN NYC
    12 APRIL 2015


    Everyone loves brunch. But, brunch in NYC can be difficult. Especially when it comes to choosing the perfect one for your group of friends. Cuisine, location, inside/outside, prices, etc. are all considered. There's always the option of eating at home or making a home made brunch... but that's not the point here! Our team has rounded up the top 5 brunch spots in NYC for different types of people and feels. Find the one that fits you best, and brunch on.

    5 Different brunch options in NYC:
    1). Westville: A cozy spot with great eggs benedict
    2). Bagatelle: Trendy nightlife brunch for people who want to party through the day
    3). Grey Dog: Small but friendly joint with huge portions
    4). Rosemary's: Elegant French cafe with beautiful outdoor garden
    5). Kitti Chai: Chic all-you-drink thai fushion brunch


    Written by: Alice Lim
