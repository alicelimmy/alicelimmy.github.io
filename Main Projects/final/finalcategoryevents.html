    New Wine Tastings Coming This Month


    Free wine is the best wine. Come out to wine week with participating restaurants. From Uptown to LES, there are hundreds of places we've curated that are participating in this event. Find that perfect merlot for your christmas party or that crisp riesling for your outdoor summer BBQ. Wine pairing classes will even be offered at some locations for those interested in different cheeses' and meats.

    Celebrate Ice Cream Day with NYC Truck Vendors


    It's almost May which means the winter is finally in the past. Although it's not short season yet, its never too early for ice cream. Make sure to grab your free Ice Cream for National Ice Cream Day from participating Vendors! And although the big names are great, try checking out some of the smaller local shops around town and all the unique flavors they have to offer.

    Monthly Home Made Cocktails Lessons


    Summer's here.... which means outdoor parties and BBQ's. Get creative with your summer party menu with some artisinal cocktails. NYC Nightlife is collaborting with some of the best bartenders in NYC to bring you a summer cocktail class. This class will teach you the basics of mixing along with fruit inspired cocktails.
